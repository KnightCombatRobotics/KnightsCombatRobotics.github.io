About Us

We're UCF's premiere combat robotics club, dedicated to excellence in everything we do

What We Do

We design, build, and compete with combat robots in various weight classes. From 1lb antweights to 3lb beetleweights, we create robots to dominate the competition.

Competition Focus

We host internal antweight competitions and fund talented makers to compete in beetleweight competitions across the United States, representing UCF on the national stage.

Join the Team

Whether you're an experienced engineer or completely new to robotics, we welcome all skill levels. Join our Discord to get involved and start building with us!
